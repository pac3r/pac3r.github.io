Stridelightby aym

A wearable platform for gamified walking

A new way to engage patients in supervised walking

Stridelight combines floor-stabilised projection with precise movement sensing, lighting, sound and scoring to create responsive, goal-driven walking sessions.

Where could this be useful?

We're still discovering the answer.

Different clinicians have already suggested applications ranging from stroke rehabilitation and Parkinson's disease to pediatrics, orthopedic recovery and research.
